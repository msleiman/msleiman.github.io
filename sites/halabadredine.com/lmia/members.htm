Regular member:

may be:

  • Any medical practitioner registered with the Lebanese Syndicate of Medicine who is a permanent resident of Lebanon who expresses an interest in, and supports the aims of the Association.
  • Any member of any paramedical or allied profession, or any member of a profession pertaining to the field of Informatics who is a permanent resident of Lebanon and who expresses an interest in, and supports the aims of the association.

Corresponding Members:

may be:

  • Any medical practitioner or scientist involved in the field of Informatics who is a permanent resident of a country other than Lebanon, who has an interest in informatics as applied to medicine, and who expresses an interest in, and supports the aims of the association.
